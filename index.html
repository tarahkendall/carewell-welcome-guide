FTO quick rules

  • Double the notice: Taking 2 weeks? Give 4 weeks notice. Taking 2 days? Give 4 days notice.
  • Max 2 consecutive weeks at a time.
  • Max 5 days in your first 90 days of employment.
  • All time off must be approved by your manager via Rippling before it counts.
  • Set up coverage — don't leave your team without what they need while you're out.
  • Block your calendar and set an out-of-office auto-reply in Gmail.
  • FTO is not accrued, not carried over, and not paid out at termination.
